Branche of Chemistry

Organic Chemistry-Study of Compounds containing carbon primarly hydrocarbons and their deravatives
Inorganic Chemistry-Study of inorganic compounds including metals,minerals and organometallic compounds.
Physical Chemistry-Branch of chemistry that uses the principles of physics to understand the behavior of matter at the molecular and atomic level, and how matter interacts
Industrial Chemistry-Branch of chemistry that uses the principles of physics to understand the behavior of matter at the molecular and atomic level, and how matter interacts
Analytical Chemistry-The branch of chemistry concerned with the separation, identification, and quantification of substances
Theratical Chemistry-Applies mathematical and computational tools to understand the structural, dynamic, and energetic properties of molecules and materials, often using quantum mechanics
Biochemistry-The study of the chemicals and chemistry of living organisms.
