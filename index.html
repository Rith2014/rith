Branches of Science

There are Many branches of science which includes biology,chemistry,physics

Biology- Biology is the scientific study of living organisms, their origins, anatomy, morphology, physiology, behavior, and distributing.
It encompasses a vast range of disciplines, from the molecular level to the global scale of ecosystems. Biology aims to understand how life began, how organisms develop, function, and interact with their environment.

Chemistry-Chemistry is a branch of natural science that deals principally with the properties of substances, the changes they undergo, and the natural laws that describe these changes.

Physics-Physics is a fundamental science that studies the nature of matter and its interactions with energy, exploring the universe from the smallest particles to the largest structures. It's concerned with understanding the fundamental laws governing motion, forces, space, and time, as well as the behavior of matter in different states and under various conditions.
